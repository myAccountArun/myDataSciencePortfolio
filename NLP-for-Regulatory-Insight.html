🧠 FinFormers: NLP for Regulatory Insight

Capstone Project | University of Cambridge | June 2025

🚀 Project Overview

As part of a six-member team, I led the development of FinFormers, a hybrid NLP and analytics solution designed to extract regulatory insight from unstructured financial communications of Global Systemically Important Banks (G-SIBs). We focused on quarterly earnings transcripts and presentations from JPMorgan Chase and UBS, surfacing early risk indicators, sentiment shifts, and potential disclosure mismatches beyond traditional financial metrics.

🎯 Objectives

  • Identify evolving executive themes and sentiment across quarters
  • Detect evasive language and selective disclosure in Q&A sessions
  • Compare spoken vs. written messaging for consistency
  • Build a scalable, end-to-end system for supervisory insight

🧩 My Contributions

  • Data Engineering & NLP Pipeline: Automated transcript extraction using PyMuPDF, regex, and GPT-4o-mini; implemented topic modelling, sentiment analysis, and avoidance detection
  • RAG Architecture & Agentic Framework: Designed a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline and multi-agent workflow using OpenAI Agents SDK
  • Dashboarding & Visualization: Built a Power BI dashboard with sentiment, topic volatility, and financial metrics; developed Early Warning Indicator (EWI)
  • Validation & Evaluation: Manually validated NLP outputs and benchmarked topic modelling approaches

📊 Key Features

  • Interactive Power BI Dashboard: Visualises sentiment trends, topic heatmaps, and avoidance ratios
  • Earnings Transcript Chatbot: GPT-powered chatbot for real-time exploration of earnings data
  • Early Warning Indicator (EWI): Flags quarters of elevated risk using NLP and market signals
